How to start?

Create your account in our site https://merchant.astropay.com/sign_up. After the registration is finished fill your account details in the panel: "Settings -> Merchant Account" and "Request to go live".

Contact ronit@astropay.com requesting to activate Rounders for the registered Merchant account.

You will be able to request payments manually to your customers in https://rounders.astropaygroup.com.
